Meet Our Founder & Your Guide

D. Nagaraja Rao

At Healing Essence, we are dedicated to harnessing the incredible power of the mind and spirit to heal, transform, and help individuals realize their fullest potential.

Founded by D. Nagaraja Rao, our practice specializes in Hypno-Healing and Past Life Regression (PLR). With over 25 years of experience in the IT industry, Mr. Rao has held prominent Senior Management roles contributing his expertise to multiple renowned organizations.

His journey into the world of healing is deeply rooted in a passion for empowering others and fostering personal transformation. Our mission is to make this knowledge reachable to everyone.

With over 15 years of experience in providing training and coaching, our services are designed to help you access your inner wisdom, release emotional blockages, and uncover the root causes of life’s challenges.

Clinical Hypnotherapy is a safe, evidence-based therapeutic modality that uses guided hypnosis to access the subconscious mind. In a deeply relaxed yet fully aware state, we gently identify and transform the root causes of emotional blocks, limiting beliefs, anxiety, phobias, and unwanted patterns. It is recognized by leading health organizations worldwide and has been used effectively for decades to support personal growth, stress reduction, and lasting behavioral change. Unlike stage hypnosis, you remain in complete control throughout the session.

Clinical Hypnotherapy works by guiding the mind into a deeply relaxed state, allowing direct access to the subconscious where emotions, habits, and automatic body responses are stored. In this state we can release stored stress and reframe limiting patterns, activating the body’s natural healing responses. It is not a replacement for medical treatment but can effectively support common stress-related and psychosomatic conditions such as anxiety, panic attacks, insomnia, migraines, IBS, phobias, smoking cessation, and chronic emotional blocks linked to pain or weight issues. Results depend on your commitment to follow through. We will discuss your specific concern during the discovery call.

Past Life Regression is a specialized form of clinical hypnosis that gently guides you to explore subconscious memories and experiences that may be influencing your present life. Using deep relaxation techniques, we create a safe space for you to recall and understand past-life or significant subconscious events that could be at the root of unexplained fears, relationship patterns, emotional blocks, or recurring challenges. The process is never forced — you remain fully aware and in control. Many clients experience profound clarity, emotional release, and healing once these deeper layers are gently addressed.
